# Automation difficulty as Archie intelligence evidence

Automation is not merely an operations surface. Every retry, correction, human override, rollback, pause, environment change, long context dependency, and failed recovery is evidence about the faculties a useful local model lacks.

`scripts/maker-archie-automation-difficulty.mjs` converts provenance-bound automation or Archie trajectory episodes into two artifacts:

1. an `archie-automation-difficulty/v1` receipt describing intervention, recovery, horizon, context, tool, environment, ambiguity, failure, and recurrence pressure;
2. an `archie-launch-sizing-hypothesis/v1` candidate model class and architecture hypothesis.

The sizing output is intentionally speculative science rather than a product claim. Low-friction repeated automations may justify a small specialist. Sustained human rescue, long-horizon recovery, broad tools, cross-environment transfer, and heavy context can raise the candidate class through compact generalists and mid-heavy models to a 20B–70B total-parameter dense or sparse-mixture hypothesis. It never admits that model. Multiple candidate sizes and architectures must still be trained and compared on hidden held-out capability, intervention reduction, safety, latency, memory, cost, retention, adaptation, and device evidence.

## Distillation implications

The same episodes determine how learning should occur:

- verified trajectory SFT for completed corrected work;
- preference distillation from human overrides and accepted corrections;
- negative unlikelihood and suppression from failed, unsafe, rejected, and rolled-back actions;
- process and verifier distillation from independent checks, stop decisions, rollback triggers, and authority gates;
- difficulty curriculum ordered by intervention, recovery, horizon, context, and environment rather than chronology alone;
- retrieval and memory distillation for recurrent and long-context work.

This signal supplements Sidepus diet provenance, admitted Maker trajectories, hidden evaluation, and device evidence. It does not replace any of them.
