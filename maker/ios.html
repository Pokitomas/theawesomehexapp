Install Archie on iPhone or iPad

  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button ⇧.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add. Archie will appear with your other apps.

What is real today

  • Archie: substantial runtime, corpus, evaluation, artifact-security, campaign, calibration, and action-contract infrastructure.
  • Not yet proven: no independent exact-head benchmark receipt currently establishes strong general task competence.
  • Sideways: the desktop corpus and archive workspace. Its factual substrate can be reused or transformed into Archie training and evaluation material with provenance intact.

Archie stores local drafts and verified receipts on this device and reads public GitHub status. Repository writes, model execution, training spend, merge, and deployment still require the authorized runtime and its explicit authority boundaries.
