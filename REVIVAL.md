## Parallel execution rule

1. A lane reads existing issues, branches, pull requests, and current main before editing.
2. A lane posts one branch claim and exact owned paths.
3. Distinct lanes may work simultaneously.
4. Overlap is recorded as a collision and resolved explicitly; it is not silently overwritten.
5. Child pull requests remain draft until their exact heads have complete receipts.
6. Assembly transports reviewed child heads; it does not recreate their work on the integration branch.
7. No model, comment, green workflow, or coordination receipt grants merge or deployment authority.
