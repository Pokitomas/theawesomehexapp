# Product separation decision

The earlier public-intelligence draft was discarded before admission because it incorrectly merged Sideways into the Archie/Maker product.

The admitted direction is:

- Archie and Maker are the intelligence-and-execution system.
- Sideways is an independent application and ordinary Maker target.
- Sideways may serve as a self-hosting reference application without becoming Archie memory, Maker infrastructure, or a privileged mutation path.
- Shared Archie intelligence improves only through explicit evaluated releases, not ambient usership.
- Intelligence and embodiment are admitted together from evidence; a weak shell cannot substitute for a strong brain, and a strong brain without the required usable access cannot represent the maximal launch.
- Product surfaces derive from demanded human outcomes. Chat, voice, background continuity, visual workbenches, tool bridges, device handoff, and local operation are faculties or adapters—not permanent architectural identities.

The durable sources are:

- `ARCHIE_MAKER_VISION.md` — product thesis and first impossible proof;
- `founder/archie-maker-vision.json` — executable product separation and release invariants;
- `founder/archie-launch-target.json` — founder intelligence and human-outcome thresholds;
- `scripts/archie-launch-contract.mjs` — deterministic joint launch evaluator.

PR #423 was closed unmerged because it treated Archie and Sideways as two surfaces of one product split.
