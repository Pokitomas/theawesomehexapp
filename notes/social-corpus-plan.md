# Social corpus pass

Replace half of the candidate surface with social-native records while preserving the automatic saturation policy.

Planned sources:

- archive journalism: English Wikinews, filtered to 2009–2019 and youth-relevant cultural/technology/economic topics;
- social-native layer: reproducible historical public-discussion records and synthetic interaction metadata, with source-specific attribution and explicit prototype labeling;
- current-2010s-revival layer: deterministic bridge records that connect archived 2010s artifacts to present-day revival motives without pretending archival text is current.

The feed must distinguish archive publication date, resurfacing date, and synthetic ranking metadata. It must not present one million candidates as one million unique posts.
