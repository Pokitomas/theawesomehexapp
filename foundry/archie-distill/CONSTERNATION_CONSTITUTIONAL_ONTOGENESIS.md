## Local compression attempt

The blueprint was compressed to its smallest defensible executable object:

1. expose only action-observation history from a partially observed microecology;
2. reconstruct predictive causal states by grouping histories with equivalent action-conditioned futures;
3. use the induced states to control interventions;
4. rebuild the distinction after a surface-symbol permutation;
5. consolidate the concept graph without retaining episodes;
6. derive a second-order returnability observable;
7. fork the concept genealogy after an unannounced causal-law change;
8. compare against an ablation using only the current raw observation.

Smoke passed. The three-seed court failed.

The full receipt is stored beside this document.

## What genuinely worked

- Predictive histories reconstructed hidden state almost perfectly under two independent surface encodings.
- The induced concept graph could be serialized, episodes removed, and intervention decisions reproduced exactly.
- A concept genealogy and revision artifact could be emitted.

These establish that causal-state reconstruction and durable semantic organization are executable ingredients.

## Why the kernel still failed

- On one seed, the concept-aware and raw-observation planners selected identical actions, so the invented observable was not a developmental cause.
- The behavioral ablation did not reliably improve across all seeds.
- The post-shift revision did not reliably outperform the archived concept.
- The alleged descendant concept, `returnability`, was written by the engineer. It was not invented by the learner.
- The planner's viability and novelty terms were written by the engineer. The developmental polity therefore remained a hidden teacher.
- Perfect hidden-state purity was not a breakthrough: the world and history depth made causal reconstruction easy.
- Genealogy records were administrative metadata; competence did not have to execute through them.

The failure is not repaired by more daemons, critics, memory types, teacher tokens, compute or prose.

## Exact surviving kernel

The only surviving direction is **instrument genesis**:

> Search over executable measurement programs, accept them only when they improve held-out counterfactual prediction and intervention across independently rendered worlds, and require accepted programs to compose into later measurement programs that alter behavior.

The measurement language may inherit primitive operations, but it must not contain the target distinction or a handcrafted descendant such as `returnability`.

A credible successor must include:

- an executable measurement-program language;
- mutation, composition and retirement of programs;
- a vector court for prediction, intervention, transport, description length and interference;
- no named semantic target;
- causal ablation of every accepted program;
- forced execution through accepted programs rather than metadata-only genealogy;
- an independently authored post-development world;
- a descendant program produced by composition, not by a hardcoded callback;
- revision through program fork or scope narrowing after counterevidence.

## Teacher entry

Teacher entry becomes scientifically meaningful only after the local program ecology produces one causally active, transported, consolidated, revised and fecund measurement without teacher proposals.
