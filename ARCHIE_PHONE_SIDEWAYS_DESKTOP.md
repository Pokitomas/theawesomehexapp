# Archie phone / Sideways desktop

## Product decision

Archie is the installable phone product. Sideways is the desktop-first web corpus, archive, and factual-workspace product.

This is a surface and authority split, not a claim that Archie is already a strong general model.

## Archie

Archie owns the phone cockpit for:

- local planning and route selection;
- evidence and action-contract inspection;
- corpus, pack, campaign, calibration, checkpoint, and learning receipts;
- command packet export to an authorized local runtime;
- Maker task authoring and public execution-state inspection.

The phone browser does not claim that training, model execution, repository mutation, synchronization, merge, deployment, or external spend happened locally. Those claims require a fresh authenticated runtime receipt.

Archie is not yet independently proven to be a strong model. Its runtime, corpus, evaluation, artifact security, campaign, calibration, and action-contract systems are substantial, but no current independent exact-head benchmark receipt establishes strong task competence.

## Sideways

Sideways remains the desktop-first personal archive and broad factual corpus workspace. It retains:

- the private local archive;
- import, provenance, deduplication, backup, and recovery;
- the bounded public factual projection and ranking laboratory;
- source-native documents, media, social exports, and structured records;
- the same inspectable ranking kernel and factual lineage.

Sideways factual material may be transformed into Archie training, negative-training, evaluation, retrieval, or campaign material only through explicit provenance-preserving pack and receipt boundaries. Sideways is the clay; Archie is the learning and action surface. Private material does not silently become training data.

## User entrypoints

- Install Archie on iPhone or iPad: `https://pokitomas.github.io/theawesomehexapp/maker/ios.html`
- Open Archie: `https://pokitomas.github.io/theawesomehexapp/maker/`
- Open Sideways desktop: `https://pokitomas.github.io/theawesomehexapp/manual/`
- Open the public factual root feed: `https://pokitomas.github.io/theawesomehexapp/`

## Admission boundary

A future Archie strength claim requires an independent exact-head evaluation receipt bound to:

- the exact model artifact and runtime ABI;
- hidden or procedurally generated tasks;
- action-contract, authority, and terminal-artifact checks;
- cost, latency, memory, and failure receipts;
- contamination and teacher-dependence controls;
- alternate valid solution paths rather than one privileged reference trace.
